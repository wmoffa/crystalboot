Crystal Collector


You will be given a random number at the start of the game.
There a four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will; add a specific amount of points to your total score.
You win the game by matching your score with the random number.
You lose the gane if your score is higher than the random number. The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it to find its value.
Each time the game restarts, the game will change the value of each crystal.
